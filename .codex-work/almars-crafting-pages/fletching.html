Classic EQ Fletching Guide

Fletching is a popular Tradeskill amongst Rangers and pullers since they need arrows the most. However, anyone can go about raising their Fletching skill. Fletching can make more than just arrows, there are many different types of bows that are made from Fletching as well. Whatever you're raising the skill for, the first 200 or so points will be quite easy for you to do since all of the materials you will be using are vendor purchased. If you're just starting out Fletching, as with every Tradeskill, you will want to visit your Guildmaster and raise the skill to 21 before starting.

 

Fletching 1 - 21

If desired you can speak with your Guildmaster to raise Fletching the first 21 points.

 

 

(Direct Route) Class 1 Bone Point Arrow 21 - 68

All four of the items you need to make Class 1 Bone Point Arrows are usually sold by the Fletching vendor in your races home town.

Bundled Bone Arrow Shafts

Field Point Arrowheads

Large Groove Nocks

Several Round Cut Fletchings
